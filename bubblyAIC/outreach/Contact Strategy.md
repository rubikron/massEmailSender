# Contact Strategy

## Segmentation

### By Role
- **Research Mentor** — Primary target for research mentor role. Identified from CS/engineering labs. Need to expand beyond CS.
- **Grad Students** — Pool for team leads and technical advisors.
- **Undergrads** — Undergraduate researchers. Can be team lead candidates.
- **Business/Econ** — Contacts from business school departments. Potential for AI+business research.

### By Track
- **Tech** (CS/Engineering): — LLM research, ML systems, HCI, NLP, robotics
- **Health** (Medicine/Genomics): — genomics, computational biology, neuroscience
- **Economy** (Business/Econ): — management, marketing, IS, finance, strategy

### By Lab
- Varies by institution — segment contacts by research group/lab affiliation
- Largest labs typically yield the most contacts
- Look for labs with active publication records in AI-adjacent fields

## Outreach Sequencing
1. Research Mentor advisors (highest leverage, ~4 needed)
2. Technical ML advisors (2–3 industry practitioners)
3. Team leads (from grad student pool)

## Tools Available
- WebSearch — Research Mentor discovery, publication research
- WebFetch — Research Mentor page scraping for personalization
- GitHub CLI — Open-source research projects
